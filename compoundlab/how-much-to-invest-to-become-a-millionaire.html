How much do I need to invest to become a millionaire?

Becoming a millionaire might sound unrealistic, but with consistent investing and time, it’s more achievable than most people think.

The simple answer

The amount you need to invest depends on three things:

  • How much you invest each month
  • Your average annual return
  • How long you stay invested

Example scenarios

Here’s how different monthly investments can grow over time at an 8% average return:

  • £200/month → ~£300,000 in 30 years
  • £400/month → ~£600,000 in 30 years
  • £650/month → ~£1,000,000 in 30 years

Start earlier, not bigger

Time is more powerful than trying to invest huge amounts. Starting earlier allows compound growth to do the heavy lifting.
