How Long Will It Take to Reach FIRE?

One of the most common questions in the FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement is:

“How long will it take me to reach financial independence?”

The answer depends on a few key factors — but with the right approach, you can estimate your timeline with surprising accuracy.

---

The Key Factors That Affect Your Timeline

Your journey to FIRE is influenced by:

  • Your current savings
  • Your monthly investment amount
  • Your investment returns
  • Your annual expenses
---

Your FIRE Number

Before estimating your timeline, you need a target.

FIRE Number = Annual Expenses × 25

---

Example Timeline

Here’s a simplified example:

  • Starting with £10,000
  • Investing £500 per month
  • Average return of 7%

In this scenario, it could take around 20–25 years to reach financial independence.

Your actual timeline may be shorter or longer depending on your situation.

How to Reach FIRE Faster

  • Increase your monthly investments
  • Reduce unnecessary expenses
  • Maximise tax-efficient accounts like ISAs
  • Stay consistent over the long term
---

What Slows You Down

  • Low savings rate
  • Taking money out of investments early
  • Not investing consistently
  • Underestimating expenses
