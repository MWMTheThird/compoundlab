Best ISA strategy UK (2026)

ISAs are one of the most powerful tools for building wealth in the UK. Here’s how to use your £20,000 allowance properly and maximise long-term growth.

What is an ISA?

An Individual Savings Account (ISA) allows you to invest or save money tax-free. This means you don’t pay tax on interest, dividends or capital gains.

Your ISA allowance

In the UK, you can invest up to £20,000 per tax year into ISAs. This allowance resets every year in April.

The best ISA strategy

  • Invest early in the tax year
  • Use a Stocks & Shares ISA
  • Invest monthly
  • Hold long-term (10+ years)

Cash vs Stocks & Shares ISA

Cash ISAs are safer but offer lower returns. Stocks & Shares ISAs provide higher long-term growth.
