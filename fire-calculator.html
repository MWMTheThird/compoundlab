How it works

What is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. The basic idea is to build enough invested wealth so that your portfolio can support your annual spending using a sustainable withdrawal rate.

A common starting point is the 4% rule, which implies a FIRE target of roughly 25 times your annual expenses. Lower withdrawal rates mean a higher target, but often a larger margin of safety.

This calculator is for educational planning only and does not guarantee actual investment returns or retirement outcomes.
