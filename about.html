About

Built to simplify long-term financial planning

CompoundLab is a modern finance calculator website built for investors, savers and anyone who wants clearer answers about money.

Why CompoundLab exists

The goal is simple: make wealth-building tools fast, intuitive and genuinely useful. Whether you are estimating the future value of monthly investing, testing different savings targets or planning your path toward £1 million, CompoundLab gives you clean tools and practical explanations without unnecessary complexity.

What comes next

The site is structured so you can expand into FIRE, mortgage, loan, dividend and inflation calculators later without redesigning everything from scratch.
