Monthly investing strategy guide

Learn how consistent investing can build long-term wealth without needing large upfront capital.

Why monthly investing works

Investing regularly helps smooth out market volatility and removes the pressure of trying to time the market perfectly.

Dollar-cost averaging

By investing the same amount each month, you naturally buy more when prices are low and less when prices are high.

Build the habit

The real power of monthly investing is consistency. Over time, small contributions can grow significantly through compounding.

Keep it simple

Most investors benefit from sticking to simple, diversified funds rather than trying to pick individual stocks.
